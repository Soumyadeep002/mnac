Practice Camp 2025
Venue: Kakiburu Hill, Purulia
Date: 9th – 10th March, 2025

About the Plan:

After the successful completion of our third rock climbing course, Makaut Nature and Adventure Club is thrilled to announce an exciting Practice Rock Climbing Camp at Kakiburu Hill, Purulia. This 2-day, 1-night adventure is designed for passionate climbers who want to refine their skills, gain hands-on experience, and immerse themselves in the thrill of rock climbing.

Kakiburu Hill, known for its challenging terrain and breathtaking views, offers the perfect setting for this camp. Participants will have the opportunity to practice under expert guidance, tackle real rock faces, and enhance their climbing techniques in a natural environment. Safety will be our top priority, with experienced instructors ensuring proper guidance and equipment support throughout the sessions.

Route: Howrah - Adra - Kantari - Adra - Howrah

Transport: Howrah-Howrah

Duration: 01N/02D

Required: Adventurus Spirit only.

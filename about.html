About Us

At Makaut Nature and Adventure Club, we ignite a passion for the outdoors and fuel the spirit of adventure in every member. Since our journey began in September 2022 with our inaugural RCC camp at the stunning Susunia Hills, we've blossomed into a vibrant community for those eager to explore, experience, and protect nature.

We provide a dynamic platform where students can connect, embark on thrilling adventures, and create unforgettable memories and friendships. Through outdoor exploration and environmental stewardship, we inspire a deeper bond with the natural world, nurturing a lifelong sense of wonder and adventure. Join us as we continue to explore the beauty of nature and embrace the thrill of the great outdoors!

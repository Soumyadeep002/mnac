ADVENTURE FEST 2025
Venue: Mathaburu Hill, Purulia
Date: 20th – 23rd December, 2025

WHY ADVENTURE FEST 2025?

Adventure Sports is not just about thrills—it's about personal growth, escape from digital overload, sustainable travel, cultural immersion, and economic development. It’s a conscious choice for those who want more than just sightseeing—a chance to challenge themselves and connect deeply with nature

Still if you feel- I have never done rock climbing or trekking. So why should participate in the Adventure Fest? Or, my children are studying. They will never do all these Adventure Activities, so why should I send them?

The answer is that we ourselves are not properly acquainted with our capabilities. This wide assortment of activities here gives us the opportunity to test our capabilities and powers in real situations which in turn boosts our morale. It gives the ability to surpass our self in real world. It gives children and teenagers a feeling of the true form, taste, smell and touch of nature, instead of showing the AI generated replica of nature on their mobile phone wallpapers.

Each activity have been curated with great care and a far-reaching vision. Each activity gives courage to face various adverse situations of life but does not suffer from the rigidity of rules in spite of being in the bounds of discipline. So, pack your rucksack, tie your shoe-lace and hop on the wagon with The MNAC.

🌿 Why Mathaburu Hills?

Nestled in the Ayodhya Range, Purulia — Mathaburu Hills is a true paradise for adventure lovers. With rocky terrains, waterfalls, forests, vibrant flora & fauna, and cultural richness, it offers the perfect backdrop for exploration and self-discovery.

💡 Why Adventure Fest?

Because adventure is more than thrill — it’s about:

  • ✨ Exploring new terrains & cultures
  • ✨ Best Exposure for First Timers to acquired new Tastes
  • ✨ Building stamina & resilience
  • ✨ Developing courage & self-confidence
  • ✨ Learning teamwork & leadership
  • ✨ Creating unforgettable stories under the open sky

1st Nature Study and Adventure Camp 2022
Venue: Susunia Hills, Bankura
Date: 29th December, 2022 - 1st january, 2023

From the Desk of the CAMP COMMANDANT:

Few students of MAKAUT, with passionate love for nature and a zeal for adventure, activities have been feeling the need of a platform/ association of their own to share and exchange ideas, experiences and enjoy together the thrill of adventure activities. Under the guidance of their mentor, Mr. Shambo Chatterjee, Asstt. Professor of MAKAUT they formed the MAKAUT Nature and Adventure Club (MNAC) in 2022.

The aim of MNAC is to promote Mountaineering and allied Adventure Activities and to build Environmental Awareness. In spite of a meager fund, lack of experience, MNAC successfully completed its maiden venture, the “1st Rock Climbing and Nature Study Camp”, during 29/12/2022 to 01/01/2023 at Susunia Hills, Bankura in collaboration with “Foransa” who provided the logistic and technical support. And thereafter, the journey goes on. It was not always smooth sailing - many an obstacle appeared to hinder their progress; but they unitedly negotiated all. Dr. Atri Bhowmik, Finance Officer has been playing a positive role from the very beginning as a valuable advisor. Mr. Subhendu Mandal and some prominent mountaineers have also lent their helping hand.
